Divided into 3 different TMA's, Baghdad Approach provides approach services below FL235 throughout the whole of the Baghdad FIR.

Baghdad Approach ensures surveillance-based separation to aircraft departing, arriving, or transiting the Ali, Baghdad, or Kirkuk TMA sectors. This section of the SOP outlines ensures a safe and efficient flow when operating a Baghdad Approach position.

The following table dictates what airports each approach unit provides top-down ATC service for.

| Logon Callsign | Frequency | Sector                   | Aerodromes |
| -------------- | --------- |--------------------------| ---------- |
| ALI_APP | 120.200 | [Ali Sector](ALI.md)     | ORMM, ORNI |
| BGD_APP | 122.400 | [Baghdad Sector](KIR.md) | ORBI |
| KIR_APP | 127.700 | [Kirkuk Sector](KIR.md)  | ORER, ORKK, ORSJ |
